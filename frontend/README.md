TestWise ek pure technology intermediary platform hai jo India mein diagnostic healthcare aur medicine information ko accessible banana chahta hai — bina khud koi medical service provide kiye. Lab Booking: TestWise ka core kaam hai NABL/ISO certified partner laboratories ko patients se connect karna. Jab koi user platform pe aata hai, woh apna test search karta hai, apne city mein available labs dekhta hai, price compare karta hai, aur booking karta hai. Yahan TestWise sirf ek digital bridge hai — test khud lab karta hai, sample lab leta hai, report lab banata hai, aur payment bhi directly patient aur lab ke beech hoti hai. TestWise beech mein na paisa leta hai, na report dekhta hai, na koi medical involvement hai. Order ID System: Jab booking hoti hai, TestWise ek Order ID generate karta hai jisme sirf teen cheezein store hoti hain — kaun si lab select ki, kya test book kiya, aur kitna amount tha. How, when, where, ya payment method — kuch store nahi hota. Agar patient ko receipt chahiye toh woh lab se directly maangega, aur agar koi problem aaye toh TestWise Order ID ke through lab tak patient ko connect karta hai — bas itna. Medicine Comparison Engine: TestWise ka doosra feature hai medicine composition matching. Agar kisi doctor ne ek branded medicine likhi hai aur patient sasta alternative dhundh raha hai, toh TestWise same salt composition wali doosri medicines dikhata hai — sirf informational basis pe. Saath mein external links bhi hote hain jaise 1mg ya NetMeds ya brand ki apni website, taaki user wahan jaake khud khareed sake. TestWise khud koi medicine sell nahi karta, dispense nahi karta, aur kisi brand ko recommend bhi nahi karta — sirf neutral composition data aur traffic link deta hai. Har jagah clearly likha hota hai ki doctor se consult kiye bina koi medicine mat badlo. Revenue Model: TestWise ka business model partnership based hai. Partner labs listing fee ya per-booking commission de sakte hain. Medicine brands apni compositions list karwa ke traffic le sakte hain. Investors platform mein invest kar sakte hain. Aur future mein corporate bulk booking ka scope bhi hai jahan companies apne employees ke liye tests book karein. Legal Position: TestWise Information Technology Act 2000 ke under ek intermediary hai — matlab woh sirf ek conduit hai, active participant nahi. Isliye medical errors ki liability lab pe hai, payment disputes ki liability lab pe hai, aur medicine reactions ki liability patient aur doctor pe hai. TestWise kabhi "recommend" nahi karta, kabhi "advise" nahi karta, aur kabhi emergency use ke liye nahi hai — 102/108 ke liye clearly redirect karta hai. DPDP Act 2023 ke under minimum data collect hota hai aur koi medical data permanently store nahi hota. Ek line mein: TestWise woh platform hai jo patient aur lab ke beech booking ka kaam karta hai, same composition wali sasti medicines dhundhne mein madad karta hai, aur poori process mein khud koi medical, financial, ya clinical role nahi leta — sirf technology ka kaam karta hai.
